Issue Stances

Where does Dusk stand on the issues of today?

Protecting everyone's pensions is going to be my first priority. I understand that many people depend on their retirement benefits to survive, and I am going to fight the reduction of pension payments, despite recent efforts by our governor to take pension payments away from hard working Americans.

I fully support the organization planned parenthood and the services they provide. They will be fully protected under the law while I am in office.

Our roads in Middlesex County have fallen into disarray and need immediate fixing. Funds will be diverted to our roads once I am in office.

New Jersey has been the victim of countless environmental disasters. Protecting our land and drinking water will be my utmost priority.

All women should have the right to an abortion, hassle free.

About


"Together we can accomplish a bold vision for reforming Middlesex County and making a better New Jersey. This will be a team effort and I can not do it alone."

Dusk Crescenzo got his start working as a field canvasser for Clean Water Action, an environmental non-profit in Long Branch, NJ.

After working there for a year, he volunteered in locations across the country for various democratic organizations.

These include Grand Forks Democrats, Ocean County Democrats, and Bernie Sanders’ Bergen County Campaign Office.

After taking a road trip to California with a group called Black Men for Bernie, he returned to New Jersey to run for office himself, inspired by Bernie’s progressive revolution.
